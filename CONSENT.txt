Zaan Koshur — Voice Contribution Consent Form
---------------------------------------------

Project Purpose:
Zaan Koshur is a Kashmiri language preservation initiative that collects
voice recordings to create speech datasets, text corpora, and language
technology tools such as ASR (speech-to-text) and TTS (text-to-speech).
All recordings support long-term cultural and linguistic preservation.

By submitting any audio recording, the contributor agrees to the
following:

1. Voluntary Participation
   I confirm that I am contributing my voice voluntarily and without any
   obligation.

2. Anonymity and Privacy
   I understand that:
   • My real name, identity, and personal details will NOT be collected.
   • My voice will remain anonymous.
   • No attempt will be made to identify me.
   • The project will not store personal information.

3. Allowed Uses
   I give permission for my voice recordings to be used for:
   • Linguistic research  
   • Speech datasets  
   • AI model training (ASR, TTS, NLP)  
   • Language preservation and educational tools  

   These uses may be public, academic, or open-source in nature.

4. Restricted Uses (Ethical Protection)
   I do NOT consent to the use of my voice for:
   • Surveillance or biometric identification  
   • Political profiling or manipulation  
   • Harassment, misinformation, or abusive systems  
   • Deepfake generation intended to mimic or impersonate me  
   • Any culturally disrespectful or harmful applications  

5. Storage and Access
   I understand that:
   • My recordings may be stored in a private or gated-access dataset.
   • Only approved researchers or collaborators may access the audio.
   • The project owner controls access permissions.

6. Right to Withdraw
   I may request removal of my recordings at any time by contacting the
   project team. Reasonable efforts will be made to delete the files and
   downstream versions where possible.

7. Ownership and License
   I grant Zaan Koshur permission to use, archive, and distribute my
   recordings under the project’s Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
   License (CC-BY 4.0) with added ethical conditions, provided my
   anonymity is preserved.

8. Agreement
   By submitting audio, I acknowledge that:
   • I have read and understood this consent form.
   • I agree to all the terms listed above.
   • I am at least 13 years old or have guardian permission.

---------------------------------------------
Thank you for supporting Kashmiri language preservation.
