Zaan Koshur — Voice Contribution Consent Form
---------------------------------------------

Project Purpose:
Zaan Koshur is a Kashmiri language preservation initiative that collects
voice recordings to support linguistic research, speech datasets, and
language technology tools such as ASR (speech-to-text) and TTS
(text-to-speech). All recordings are used exclusively for cultural,
educational, and language preservation purposes.

By submitting any audio recording, the contributor agrees to the
following:

1. Voluntary Participation
   I confirm that I am contributing my voice voluntarily and without any
   obligation, pressure, or compensation.

2. Anonymity and Privacy
   I understand that:
   • My real name, identity, and personal details will NOT be collected.
   • My voice contributions will remain anonymous.
   • No attempt will be made to identify, trace, or deanonymize me.
   • The project will not store personal identifying information.

3. Permitted Uses
   I give permission for my voice recordings to be used for:
   • Linguistic and academic research
   • Creation of speech datasets
   • Training of AI models (ASR, TTS, NLP)
   • Language preservation, documentation, and educational tools

   These uses are strictly limited to ethical, non-harmful, and
   language-preservation purposes.

4. Prohibited Uses (Ethical Protection)
   I do NOT consent to the use of my voice for:
   • Surveillance or biometric identification
   • Speaker identification or profiling
   • Political manipulation or propaganda
   • Harassment, discrimination, or misinformation
   • Deepfake systems intended to impersonate or recreate my identity
   • Any culturally disrespectful or harmful applications

5. Storage and Access
   I understand that:
   • My recordings will be stored in a private or restricted-access
     dataset.
   • Audio data is NOT publicly released or open-source.
   • Access to recordings is controlled exclusively by the project
     owner and authorized maintainers.
   • Audio files may only be shared with approved researchers or
     collaborators under strict ethical conditions.

6. Right to Withdraw
   I may request removal of my voice recordings at any time by contacting
   the project team. Reasonable efforts will be made to delete the data
   and any derived artifacts where technically feasible.

7. Ownership and License
   I grant Zaan Koshur permission to store, use, and process my voice
   recordings for the purposes stated above.

   I understand that:
   • Voice recordings are NOT released under an open public license.
   • Audio data remains restricted and is not redistributable.
   • Project documentation, code, and standards may be open-source, but
     voice recordings are excluded from public licensing.

8. Agreement
   By submitting audio, I acknowledge that:
   • I have read and understood this consent form.
   • I agree to all the terms listed above.
   • I am at least 13 years old, or I have legal guardian permission.

---------------------------------------------
Thank you for contributing to the preservation
and future of the Kashmiri language.
---------------------------------------------
